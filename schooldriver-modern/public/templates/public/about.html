    About Our School

    Excellence in education since 1985

    Our Mission

    To provide a comprehensive, innovative educational experience that prepares students for success in an ever-changing world. We are committed to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development in a supportive learning environment.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading educational institution that empowers students to become confident, compassionate, and capable global citizens who contribute positively to their communities and society at large.

    Our History

    Founded in 1985, SchoolDriver Modern has been at the forefront of educational innovation for nearly four decades. What started as a small community school has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving over 1,200 students from diverse backgrounds.

    Throughout our history, we have consistently adapted to meet the changing needs of our students and community. In 2020, we underwent a major digital transformation, implementing state-of-the-art technology and modern teaching methodologies to enhance the learning experience.

    Today, we are proud to offer a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and support services that help every student reach their full potential.
