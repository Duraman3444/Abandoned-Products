    Elementary (K-5)

    Required Documents:
    • Completed application form
    • Birth certificate
    • Immunization records
    • Previous school records (if applicable)
    • Parent/guardian identification
    Assessment:

    Age-appropriate readiness evaluation for kindergarten applicants.

    Middle School (6-8)

    Required Documents:
    • Completed application form
    • Official transcripts (2 years)
    • Teacher recommendations (2)
    • Standardized test scores
    • Immunization records
    Assessment:

    Entrance exam covering math, reading, and writing skills.

    High School (9-12)

    Required Documents:
    • Completed application form
    • Official transcripts (all years)
    • Teacher recommendations (3)
    • Personal essay
    • Extracurricular activity list
    Assessment:

    Comprehensive entrance exam and personal interview.

    Financial Aid & Scholarships

    Need-Based Financial Aid

    We believe that financial circumstances should not prevent qualified students from receiving an excellent education. Our need-based financial aid program assists families who demonstrate financial need.

    • Up to 75% tuition assistance
    • Payment plans available
    • Sibling discounts
    Merit Scholarships

    Outstanding students may qualify for merit-based scholarships based on academic achievement, leadership potential, and special talents.

    • Academic Excellence Scholarship
    • Leadership Award
    • Arts & Athletics Scholarships
