    Academic Programs

    Comprehensive education from elementary through high school

    Elementary School

    Grades K-5

    Our elementary program focuses on building strong foundational skills in reading, writing, mathematics, and science while fostering creativity and social development.

    Key Features:
    • Small class sizes (15-18 students)
    • Integrated STEAM curriculum
    • Daily physical education
    • Art and music programs
    • Character education

    Middle School

    Grades 6-8

    Our middle school program bridges elementary and high school, helping students develop independence while exploring their interests and talents.

    Key Features:
    • Departmentalized instruction
    • Advanced placement options
    • Leadership opportunities
    • Clubs and athletics
    • Peer mentoring program

    High School

    Grades 9-12

    Our high school program prepares students for college and career success through rigorous academics and extensive extracurricular opportunities.

    Key Features:
    • AP and dual enrollment courses
    • Career pathway programs
    • College counseling
    • Internship opportunities
    • Comprehensive athletics

    Special Programs & Services

    Gifted & Talented

    Advanced curriculum and enrichment activities for high-achieving students. Includes specialized classes, independent study projects, and academic competitions.

    Special Education

    Comprehensive support services for students with diverse learning needs. Individualized education plans and specialized instruction in inclusive settings.

    ESL Program

    English as a Second Language support for non-native speakers. Intensive language instruction with cultural integration activities.

    Technology Integration

    1:1 device program with integrated digital literacy curriculum. Coding, robotics, and digital design courses across all grade levels.
